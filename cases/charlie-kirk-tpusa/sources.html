Investigation Methodology

This investigation relied on multiple independent sources, cross-verification of documents, and protection of whistleblower identities. All claims are supported by verifiable evidence.

  • Analysis of IRS filings and financial documents
  • Interviews with former TPUSA employees and targeted faculty
  • Cross-referencing of leaked internal documents
  • Congressional testimony and official investigations
